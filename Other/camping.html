Guide to Camping

To begin I would reccomend following these guidelines:
  1. Choose a location that you can drive up to, to avoid hiking too far and if you decide you decide part of the way through the night you don't have hike in the dark.
  2. Make sure you check the weather and plan clothes accordingly, remember higher altitude = colder temperatures
  3. Bring a stove or firewood with lighters and Plan out meals:
    • KISS = Keep It Simple Stupid
    • Breakfast: Bacon, eggs, in tortillas
    • Lunch: Peanut Butter and Jelly in a plastic container
    • Dinner: Chicken Tacos!
  4. Bring extra water! Very important!
  5. Prepare for discomfort, sleeping on the ground is not the most fun!
  6. Look for local camping guides for more information specific to your area!
  7. Let someone know where you are camping, and go with a group if possible

Once you get there:
  1. Find your camping spot, and place permits in the correct place
  2. Begin by finding a flat space to place your tent
  3. Set up tent, make sure its a safe distance from the road and the fire pit
  4. Find firewood to keep the fire going until you are ready for bed
  5. Enjoy the night, do some exploring, and go to bed later than normal, it will help you stay asleep
  6. Make sure you pick up your trash, leave no trace
  7. Wake up, eat breakfast, clean up, and head out!
